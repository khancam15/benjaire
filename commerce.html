Ecommerce Operations

Benjaire builds ecommerce operations that can scale across multiple channels, product categories, and market conditions. The objective is steady margin growth, predictable fulfillment, and resilient systems that hold under pressure.

Commerce · Retail Operations

Channel Strategy

We design a channel mix that balances acquisition cost, margin, and operational complexity across marketplaces, direct storefronts, and partner channels.

Execution Note

Channel priorities are reviewed quarterly to match demand shifts and protect contribution margin.

Sourcing and Inventory

Supplier qualification, lead-time planning, and reorder discipline are modeled to reduce stockouts and dead inventory.

Execution Note

Inventory policy is tied to lead time, demand volatility, and service-level targets by SKU tier.

Performance Management

We monitor contribution margin, return rates, and demand velocity to prioritize decisions that improve unit economics.

Execution Note

Weekly scorecards track profitability drivers instead of vanity metrics to keep decisions grounded.

Operating Model

Our model is designed for repeatability. Each process is documented, measured, and improved over time. We avoid growth tactics that inflate top-line revenue but weaken the underlying business.

  • Standardized sourcing scorecards for quality, reliability, and landed cost.
  • Forecasting cadence with weekly demand reviews and inventory health checks.
  • Clear exception handling for listing quality, customer issues, and fulfillment delays.

What Good Looks Like

A healthy ecommerce operation is measured by durability: stable cash conversion, defensible margins, and the ability to fulfill reliably as volume grows.

Measurement Lens

We evaluate operational health through margin quality, fulfillment reliability, and cash-cycle stability.
