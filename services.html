What We Hold Ourselves To

Engagements are scoped to results. We do not bill for hours that do not move the project forward. If something is not working, we surface it early and adjust.

Every system we build ships with documentation your team can follow without us. No dependency on Benjaire for ongoing operation of what we deliver.

We flag tradeoffs clearly. We do not recommend systems or changes we would not run ourselves. High-risk proposals get honest scoping, not optimistic projections.

Direct Communication

One point of contact. Status updates that say something real. If there is a problem, you hear about it from us before it surfaces elsewhere.
